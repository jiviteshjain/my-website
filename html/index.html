An experience

...that changed me as a person.

It was my fifth year studying German when I appeared on the Fit in Deutsch 2 (A2) examination conducted by the Goethe Institute. My happiness knew no bounds and I was still partly dazed by the clouds of disbelief as I heard my name being called off the list of candidates selected for an all-expenses-paid trip to Germany.
Once there, we were enrolled in Jugendkurs Aachen 2016 - a German Language Course - along with other people from all around the world. It was three weeks of intensive learning, great fun, amazing experiences and memories to cherish.
It was on the last day that, standing by the waters of the Rhine, I could instrospect upon the impact of the course on me - it was not merely a prize, and had not just taught me the German Language - but it had made me independent and given me the exposure and confidence necessary to face the world. It had played its small part in transforming this child into a youth - justifying itself as a Jugendkurs.
